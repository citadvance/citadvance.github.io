SESSION 4


Title: Building Your Personal Brand

Speaker: Sean O'Donovan, Dell EMC

Friday February 1, 2019 15:20-16:20, Seminar Room


Abstract

This session will address the dos and don'ts of building a professional image.


About the speaker

With over 15 years Talent Acquisition experience, Sean O'Donovan has worked on both sides of the "the fence".
9 years working with DELLEMC recruiting for all Business Functions, 3 years with CPL working accounts in MNC, FMCG and SMB's. He also has 3 years HR Generalist experience. His interests lie in Diversity, STEM and building and maintaining your Brand as we now enter a new age in the Workplace Environment.
