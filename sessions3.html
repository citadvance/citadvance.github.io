SESSION 3


Interviewing - Preparations and Techniques

Speaker: Cynthia O'Keeffe, VMWare

Friday February 1, 2019 14:30-15:00, Seminar Room


Abstract

This session will focus on the process of job interviews. Practical tips and examples will be given.


About the speaker

Before moving into her current role, Cynthia O'Keeffe was the manager of a large global team in the VMWare Talent Acquisition organisation and is the ideal speaker to equip the attendees with the best knowledge on how to excel in interviews. Cynthia was also responsible for spearheading the Coder Dojo initiative within VMware and is a passionate proponent of young women striving to push themselves beyond expectations and limitations.
