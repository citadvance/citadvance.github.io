SESSION 5


Title: LinkedIn - How to Exploit It

Speaker: Louise Bunyan (sponsored by Poppulo)

Friday February 8, 2019 12:40-13:30, Laboratory


Abstract

Today's recruitment landscape is changing with 36% of recruitment now being done through social media alone. And the place where recruiters do most of their searching? LinkedIn. But with over 570m global users, how do you stand out from the crowd? This workshop will show you how..


About the speaker

Louise Bunyan, Director of SmartFox.ie, is an award-winning digital marketing consultant, social media trainer, speaker and writer based in Co. Cork. With over 15 years' experience in communications & digital marketing, a two year stint as a teacher in New Zealand, Winner of Best Marketing and Communications Blog 2016, and finalist for Best New Emerging Business, Network Cork Business Awards, Louise is one of Ireland's few LinkedIn training specialists. Through her workshops, talks and in-house corporate training, Louise and SmartFox are on a mission - to empower everyone in how to use LinkedIn to drive their own goals.
